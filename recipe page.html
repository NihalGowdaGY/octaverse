Donne Biryani

Donne Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic biryani that originates from the military hotels of Bangalore, Karnataka. The biryani gets its name from the "donne" an eco-friendly bowl.

Ingredients

  • Short-grain rice
  • Meat
  • Green masala
  • Ghee or oil

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Green Masala Paste
  2. Marinate and Cook the Meat
  3. Cook the Rice Separately
  4. Layer and Steam (Dum Cooking)
